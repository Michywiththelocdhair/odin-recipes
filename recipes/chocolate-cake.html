Chocolate Cake

Description

A vegan chocolate cake?? Soooo good. Try it!

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Cocoa powder
  • Semi-sweet chocolate
  • Sugar
  • Baking powder
  • Oil
  • Milk
  • Vinegar

Steps

  1. Whisk the oil and sugar together
  2. Mix baking powder, coca powder and flour together
  3. Add in melted chocolate to the oil sugar mix
  4. Add in the flour mixture alternating with milk for a good consistency
  5. Add in your vinegar
  6. Pour the mixture into a lined baking dish, and put it in a preheated oven for 45 minutes at 170 C
