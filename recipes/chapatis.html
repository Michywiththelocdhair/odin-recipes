Chapatis

Description

Another fave in this household, you'll love it!

Ingredients

  • Wheat flour
  • Salt
  • Oil
  • Water

Steps

  1. Mix the flour and salt
  2. Add in the oil and mix it in with your fingrtips so it breaks down
  3. Add in warm water a little at a time while mixing and kneading until it forms a soft dough. It shouldn't be sticky
  4. Cut the dough into small balls of the same size
  5. Roll out a ball into a circle and heat it on both sides on a pan
  6. Add a little bit of oil on the pan then repeat it
  7. Do the same thing for all the balls
