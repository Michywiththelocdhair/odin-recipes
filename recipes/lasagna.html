Lasagna

Description

This is probably going to be the best lasagna you have ever tasted in your entire lif. I could come up with a story about how the recipe was passed on to me by my family members, but it wasn't. This recipe took quite a few tries before it got to be perfected. Try it out and tell me what you think!

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Bell peppers
  • Soya mince
  • Lasagna sheets
  • Heavy cream
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Paprika

Steps

  1. With a little butter in a pot, cook down the onions. Once they're done, add the chopped tomatoes and let it cook down in low heat
  2. Add in the mince and bell peppers, then add spices to taste
  3. Add a little bit of water and cover with a lid
  4. In another pot, put a little bit of butter and the sour cream, and add a bit of parsley then let that thicken up
  5. Ready your lasagna dish by layig down the first layer of sheets. Then put a layer of mince, then a layer of sour cream. Do this over and over to the top
  6. Add some grated cheese at the top
  7. Cook in a preheated oven for 45 minutes at 150 C
